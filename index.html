Moving to Martuvam

Posted 12th of October 2019

Pascal and I finally moved to our new home in Auroville today. We were staying in Kuilapalayam for the past two weeks, but that was just too far away from our workplaces and the apartment was also very small for two people. Now we live in Martuvam Healing Forest in the Green Belt and I love it so far. The place is a lot bigger, has tons of storage room, and despite still being outside the inner city borders of Auroville, is a lot more central than Kuilapalayam.
